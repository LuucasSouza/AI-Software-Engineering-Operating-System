# Professional Engineer Intake Playbook

## Goal

Collect technical, product, architecture, risk and delivery context for full AI-SEOS lifecycle execution.

## Steps

1. Capture business and product context.
2. Capture functional and non-functional requirements.
3. Capture technical constraints.
4. Identify architecture assumptions.
5. Identify domain and integration candidates.
6. Identify ADR candidates.
7. Identify risk flags.
8. Assign readiness level and downstream artifacts.

## Rule

Challenge weak assumptions and premature architecture choices.
