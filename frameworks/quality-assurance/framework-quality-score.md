# Framework Quality Score

| Score | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 0 | Missing |
| 1 | Weak |
| 2 | Partial |
| 3 | Acceptable |
| 4 | Strong |
| 5 | Excellent |

## Active Status Minimum

No critical dimension below 3, average score at least 3.5, agent executability at least 3, human reviewability at least 3 and traceability at least 3.

## Stable Status Minimum

No critical dimension below 4, average score at least 4.2, adoption examples exist and quality gates are validated through reference implementation.
