# Maturity Levels

## M0 — Ad Hoc

Acceptable only for experiments, prototypes or learning exercises.

## M1 — Documented

Basic artifacts exist but are not consistently connected.

## M2 — Structured

Engines, templates, ADRs, risk register and execution plan are used.

## M3 — Integrated

Cross-engine handoffs, scorecards and traceability are present.

## M4 — Governed

Versioning, review, ownership and quality gates are enforced.

## M5 — Adaptive

Reflection drives real improvements and framework evolution.
