# Readiness Scorecards

## Generic Structure

- readiness area;
- project;
- assessed by;
- score 0-5;
- status;
- required artifacts;
- quality gates;
- risks;
- open questions;
- recommendation.

## Score Ranges

| Status | Score | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Blocked | 0-1 | Cannot proceed responsibly |
| Weak | 2 | Low-risk exploration only |
| Acceptable | 3 | Proceed with known gaps |
| Strong | 4 | Ready for professional execution |
| Excellent | 5 | Strong traceability and review |
