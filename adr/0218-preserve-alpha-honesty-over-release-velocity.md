# ADR 0218 - Preserve Alpha Honesty Over Release Velocity

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Publishing early can damage trust if users misunderstand safety or value.

## Decision

Preserve alpha honesty over release velocity.

## Consequences

- Scores may stay LIMITED GO despite technical progress.
- Public release waits for evidence.
- Known blockers stay visible.
