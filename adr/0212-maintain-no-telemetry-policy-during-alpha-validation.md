# ADR 0212 - Maintain No Telemetry Policy During Alpha Validation

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Telemetry could simplify feedback collection, but it increases privacy and trust risk.

## Decision

Maintain no telemetry during alpha validation.

## Consequences

- Feedback remains manual and consent-based.
- No external collection is added.
- Privacy posture stays simple.

## Alternatives Considered

- Add anonymous usage telemetry.
- Add event logging during sessions.
