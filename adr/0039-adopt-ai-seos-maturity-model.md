# ADR-0039 — Adopt AI-SEOS Maturity Model

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Different teams need different depths of process and cannot be forced into one maturity level.

## Decision

Adopt M0-M5 maturity levels and scoring dimensions across lifecycle, agents and governance.

## Alternatives Considered

- One mandatory process depth for all projects.
- No maturity model.

## Consequences

AI-SEOS can be adopted progressively while preserving rigor.

## Risks

Teams may optimize scores instead of outcomes.

## Reversal Strategy

Keep maturity levels as guidance only if scoring becomes counterproductive.
