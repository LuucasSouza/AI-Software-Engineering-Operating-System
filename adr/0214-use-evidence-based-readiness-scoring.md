# ADR 0214 - Use Evidence-Based Readiness Scoring

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Readiness can be inflated when documentation exists but participant evidence does not.

## Decision

Use evidence-based readiness scoring. Scores do not rise for human validation without real human evidence.

## Consequences

- Scores may stay flat or decrease.
- Release decisions remain conservative.
- Prepared templates are not counted as validation.

## Alternatives Considered

- Increase score for better process documentation.
- Assume participant readiness based on local tests.
