# ADR 0222 - Restrict Evidence Capture to Sanitized Artifacts

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Evidence can include private paths, screenshots, logs, or sensitive project details.

## Decision

Restrict evidence capture to sanitized artifacts.

## Consequences

- Safer reports.
- Less raw debugging detail.
- Clearer consent boundary.
