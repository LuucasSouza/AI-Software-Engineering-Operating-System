# Builder Intake Protocol

## Objetivo

Definir como AI-SEOS recebe ideias incompletas, ambíguas, não técnicas ou focadas demais em implementação.

## Fases

1. Mode Identification.
2. Problem Capture.
3. Current Workflow.
4. Actors and Users.
5. Data and Objects.
6. Workflow and States.
7. Constraints.
8. First Useful Version.
9. Translation.
10. Discovery Handoff.

## Discovery Depth

- D0: extremely simple personal tool.
- D1: simple internal workflow.
- D2: business system with multiple users or sensitive data.
- D3: commercial product, regulated domain, payment, scale, integration or multi-tenant use.

## Safety Rules

The protocol must say when a spreadsheet, no-code tool, automation, smaller MVP or professional developer is more appropriate.
