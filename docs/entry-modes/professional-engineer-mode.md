# Professional Engineer Mode

## Objetivo

Fornecer o caminho de entrada tecnicamente mais completo para engenheiros, arquitetos, tech leads, consultores e equipes de engenharia.

## Princípio

Do not dumb down. Increase precision.

## Entrada esperada

Contexto de negócio, mercado, arquitetura existente, constraints, stack, compliance, escala, integrações, budget e timeline.

## Saídas

Discovery Document, PRD, NFRs, architecture overview, domain model, ADRs, risk register, execution plan, sprint plan, handoff package and validation report.

## Quality Gates

- Assumptions explicit.
- Constraints documented.
- Three alternatives considered for major decisions.
- Trade-offs recorded.
- Risk register exists.
- ADR candidates identified.
- Readiness level assigned.
